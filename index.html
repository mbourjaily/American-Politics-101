American Politics 101

A civics course for anyone who wants to understand — and take part in — the American political system.

This site collects the materials for the course: the session slide decks, an AI-guided interview tool for reflecting on your own view of American politics, and the founding documents and books referenced throughout the sessions.

Two themes run through everything here: politics is accessible, and participation is patriotic. You don't need a law degree or an insider's résumé to understand self-government — the system was designed to be operated by ordinary citizens.
